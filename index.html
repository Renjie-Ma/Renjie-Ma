Areas of Research
  • Hybrid Control Systems (Hybrid Systems Lab Web Page)
    These are systems which combine continuous time dynamics with discrete event dynamics. Research involves design, verification using new computational methods for computing reachable sets, and simulation of hybrid systems. We are currently developing the theory and designing models to predict the behavior of complex systems.
    This work is supported by NSF, ONR, and AFOSR.
