Brief Bio

Renjie Ma was born in Harbin, China, in 1994. He received the B.E. degree in Automation from Hefei University of Technology, Hefei, China, in 2017. Since then, He is now working towards the PhD degree in Control Science and Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China. His research interests include cyber-physical systems security/reliability/repairability, optimal/robust control and filtering, machine learning, and applications to autonomous intelligent systems. He was the awardee of National Scholarship for Postgraduate by Ministry of Education, China and Outstanding Student Prize by Harbin Institute of Technology both in 2020.
